How It Works

Three steps to a calmer inbox

GraceBox works quietly in the background, turning hostile emails into professional communication.

Step 01

Screen

GraceBox monitors your inbox for emails from senders you flag. Uses Gmail's API for real-time detection.

Step 02

Rewrite

AI analyzes the tone, preserves the facts, and rewrites the message in clear, respectful language.

Step 03

Deliver

You receive the rewritten email in your inbox. Originals are safely stored in a private GraceBox folder for reference.
